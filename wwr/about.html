History of Rafting

Rafting: A Thrilling Adventure

Rafting, or whitewater rafting, is an exciting sport that involves navigating rivers using an inflatable raft. Originating in the 19th century for transportation, it evolved into a popular adventure activity with different levels of difficulty, from gentle streams to intense rapids.
